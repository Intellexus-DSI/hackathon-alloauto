bod skad du /
dpal ri khrod zhabs kyi bstod pa rin po che /
dpal ldan bla ma dam pa'i zhabs la phyag 'tshal lo / /
rdo rje'i rang bzhin mchog gi rdo rje sems dpa' ni / /
nam mkha'i ngo bo rnam pa thams cad mchog rang bzhin / /
'dod pa sel ba des na sha yig gis brjod pa / /
