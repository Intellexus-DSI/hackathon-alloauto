! bka' 'dri bzhi pa'i lan ni /
spyir brgyud pa'i bla ma la ltos na chos 'brel yod tshad la rtsa ba'i bla ma'i tha snyad byed pa'ang yod dngos kyi bla ma la ltos na lam rim lta bu la nyams len byed pa'i tshe /
lam rim ston pa po la rtsa ba'i bla ma'i tha snyad byed par bdag gi bla ma yongs 'dzin gu ge ba de nyid kyi zhal las gsung pa dngos su thos /
yang bka' brgyud pa sogs stong nyid ston pa po la nges don ston pa'i bla ma zhes rtsa ba'i bla mar byed pa dang /
bka' gdams pa rnams byang chub kyi sems rang rgyud la skyed mkhan la rtsa ba'i bla mar byed pa lta bu rang la chos kyi bka' drin che ba zhig la rtsa ba'i bla mar byed pa'ang yod pas mtha' gcig tu ma nges lags /
